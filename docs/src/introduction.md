# Pretorin CRM

Pretorin CRM is a customer relationship management platform purpose-built for government contracting (GovCon). It provides a complete capture management pipeline, contact tracking, teaming coordination, compliance monitoring, and Gmail integration -- everything a GovCon business development team needs in one place.

## Key Features

- **Opportunity Pipeline** -- Track opportunities through 8 stages from identification to award, with Kanban-style drag-and-drop.
- **Account & Contact Management** -- Organize government agencies, prime contractors, subcontractors, and partners with relationship tracking and follow-up reminders.
- **Capture Management** -- Structured capture notes for customer intel, competitors, incumbents, risks, and win strategy.
- **Teaming & Proposals** -- Manage teaming arrangements (prime/sub/JV) and proposal workflows per opportunity.
- **Contract Vehicles** -- Track GSA MAS, OASIS, SeaPort-NxG, and other contract vehicles.
- **Compliance Tracking** -- Monitor certifications (8(a), HUBZone, WOSB, SDVOSB) with expiration alerts.
- **Gmail Integration** -- Sync emails with contacts, send emails directly from the CRM, with automatic contact matching.
- **SAM.gov Integration** -- Import opportunities directly from SAM.gov.
- **API Access** -- Full REST API with API key authentication for integrations.
- **Google Sign-In** -- Authenticate with Google identity provider alongside traditional email/password login.
