# Dashboard

The Dashboard provides an at-a-glance overview of your pipeline and upcoming tasks.

## Pipeline Metrics

The top of the dashboard shows key performance indicators:

- **Total Opportunities** -- Number of active opportunities in the pipeline.
- **Pipeline Value** -- Sum of estimated values across all active opportunities.
- **Expected Award Revenue** -- Pipeline value weighted by win probability.
- **Win Rate** -- Percentage of opportunities in the "awarded" stage vs. total closed (awarded + lost).
- **Average Deal Size** -- Mean estimated value across opportunities.

## Stage Breakdown

A breakdown of opportunities by pipeline stage shows how many opportunities are in each stage and their combined value. Stages are:

1. **Identified** -- Initial discovery of an opportunity.
2. **Qualified** -- Evaluated and deemed worth pursuing.
3. **Capture** -- Active capture activities underway.
4. **Teaming** -- Identifying and securing teaming partners.
5. **Proposal** -- Proposal development in progress.
6. **Submitted** -- Proposal submitted, awaiting decision.
7. **Awarded** -- Contract awarded.
8. **Lost** -- Opportunity lost or no-bid.

## Follow-Up Reminders

The dashboard displays contacts with upcoming or overdue follow-ups, helping you stay on top of relationship management.

## Quick Navigation

Click on any metric, stage, or follow-up item to navigate directly to the relevant detail page.
